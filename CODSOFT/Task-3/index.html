History of Calculators

The history of calculators dates back to ancient times with the abacus. The first mechanical calculators appeared in the 17th century, invented by Blaise Pascal and Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz. In the 20th century, electronic calculators became prevalent, evolving into today's sophisticated devices with advanced functionalities.

Modern calculators can perform a wide range of functions, from basic arithmetic to complex scientific calculations. They are essential tools in education, engineering, finance, and various other fields.
